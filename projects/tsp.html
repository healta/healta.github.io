The Travelling Salesman Problem (TSP) asks: given a list of cities and distances between them, what is the shortest possible route visiting each city exactly once and returning to the origin? This is an NP-hard problem, meaning there's no known polynomial-time solution. These visualizations explore different optimization approaches.
